Everyday Healthy Smoothie

Vegan, Gluten-free, and Deliciuos

This smoothie contains many superfoods and is filling. You can make it into a smoothie bowl and add granola on top with some extra fruits for breakfast or even for a post-workout snack! I make this recipe almost everyday and have different substitutions! Hope you enjoy!

CAN YOU TASTE SPINACH IN THE SMOOTHIE? You wouldn’t be the first to look at this recipe and say “no way!” Green! Spinach! Yuck! Yet don’t walk away! This bright green gem will actually delight your taste buds. You’ll even get a head start in adding veges in your system early!

Even kids LOVE this green smoothie recipe! And my picky mother.That’s how delicious this recipe is!

To reiterate, I really encourage you to dive into the world of smoothies with added greens and see just how delicious healthy tastes.

In the smoothie realm, there are no rules! Feel free to experiment and make different fruit combinations and add any supplements you would like. Trust me. Blending them into smoothies... you can't even taste it.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup - 1 cup Oat milk (or any other nut milk)
  • 1/2 cup ice
  • 1 Banana
  • 1/2 Avacado
  • Handful of Spinach
  • 1 tsp Chia Seeds
  • 1 Tbsp Flax Seeds
  • 1 serving chocolate, vanilla, or plain protein powder of your choice
  • 1/2 tsp tumeric
  • optional: 1/2 tbsp peanut butter

Instructions

  • Add all ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth
  • Use 1/2 cup milk for a more thick consistency
  • Can add kale and any other fruits you'd like
Hope You Enjoy!
